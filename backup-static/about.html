The Studio · Est. 2017

A small studio
with big
opinions.

Bosecrafts is six people — three designers, a strategist, a writer, and an operator. We work out of a converted printing press in Siliguri's old quarter. We take on six to eight projects a year. We turn down twice as many.

Why we exist

Most agencies treat design as decoration. We treat it as infrastructure — load-bearing, slow to change, and capable of holding a business up for a decade.

We're here for founders who want to build the kind of brand they'll be proud of in ten years. Not the kind that wins awards this quarter and feels dated by next.

If that sounds like the work you want done, we'd like to hear about it.

Principles

Three things we
won't compromise on.

01

Strategy first.

Three weeks of thinking before a single pixel. The brief decides the work — not the other way around. We don't move into design until both sides have signed off.

02

Craft over speed.

Every brand we ship is reviewed against itself, twice, by someone who didn't make it. If the answer is "we can ship it tomorrow but it's 80%," we ship it next week at 100%.

03

Stay for the launch.

Six months of follow-through is baked into every engagement. We don't disappear after handover. The brand is yours to use, ours to make sure you can.
