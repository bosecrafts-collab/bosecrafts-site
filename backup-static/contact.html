FAQ

Common questions.

How long does a typical project take?

Identity projects: 8–12 weeks. Web projects: 10–14 weeks. Full rebrand with strategy + identity + web: 16–20 weeks. We'll give you a hard timeline before we start.

Do you work with brands outside India?

Yes. About 30% of our work is for clients in the UK, US, and Southeast Asia. We work async-first, with weekly sync calls scheduled in your timezone.

What's your typical project budget?

Identity-only projects start at ₹2 lakhs. Full brand + web programs sit between ₹6L and ₹15L. We're transparent about pricing — happy to share rates before you fill out a form.

Can we see your contract / process?

Yes — we'll send you a sample SOW, our process document, and three references after the initial intro call. Everything's transparent from the start.
